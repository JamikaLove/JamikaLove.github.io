The FAFSA (Federal Application for Student Aid) opens on October 1st. All seniors and their parents are encouraged to submit this document as soon as possible to ensure that students receive the maximum federal aid for college next fall. The application can be found at FAFSA.ed.gov.

The following items are needed to complete the FAFSA:

  • Student's social security number
  • Parent/Guardian's social security number
  • Parent/Guardian's dates of birth
  • Parent/Guardian's date of marriage/divorce ( if not single)
  • If parent is remarried: Step parent's social security number and date of birth
  • 2017 Tax Return

After submitting the FAFSA, please email or send your counselor a copy of your confirmation page for our records.
