In this section I will include a monthly guideline for students and their parents to follow with regard to graduation and the students post-secondary goals. I have provide an example below:

  • August- Check with your counselor to ensure that you are on track for graduation!
  • September- Research colleges and begin to narrow down your choices!
  • October- Start filling out applications and retake the ACT if you are unhappy with your score.
