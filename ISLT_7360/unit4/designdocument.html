Purpose

This website is intended for students and parents of graduating seniors to gather information that will assist them with their transition from high school to college/military/career. The purpose of this website will be to provide information on upcoming events deadlines, financial aid and other post-secondary tips. In addition to pertinent information, students and parents will be able to fill out a checklist to monitor where they are in the process.

Use Cases

  • Students and/or parents looking for a list of scholarships
  • Students and/or parents looking for testing dates and deadlines
  • Parents who wish to assist their student with the college application process but are unsure of where to start
